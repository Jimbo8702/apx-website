## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand how visitors use the Site. Cookies are small files placed on your device.

**How cookies and tracking are used:**

- To analyze website traffic and identify popular pages (via Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager).
- To remember your preferences for a more personalized experience.
- To measure the effectiveness of our content and any promotions.

**Managing cookies and analytics:**

- You can adjust your browser settings to refuse or delete cookies. Some Site features may be limited if cookies are disabled.
- You can opt out of Google Analytics across websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, available at [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
- For more information on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
